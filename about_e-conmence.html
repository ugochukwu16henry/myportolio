Our Story

Family Games Store was founded in 2018 by Sarah and Michael Johnson, two parents who struggled to find quality educational games that their children truly enjoyed. Frustrated with the limited options available, they decided to create a store that would specialize in games that are both fun and educational.

What started as a small online shop operating from their garage has grown into a trusted destination for families across the country. We've personally tested every game in our collection to ensure it meets our high standards for quality, educational value, and most importantly - fun!

Our Mission

Our mission is simple: to help families create meaningful connections through play. We believe that the best learning happens when children are engaged, curious, and having fun.

  • Curate the highest quality educational games and activities
  • Promote screen-free family time and interactive play
  • Support child development through age-appropriate challenges
  • Make learning an adventure that children look forward to
  • Build a community of families who value quality time together
