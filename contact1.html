Frequently Asked Questions

What is your shipping policy?

+

We offer free standard shipping on orders over $50. Standard shipping takes 3-5 business days, while expedited shipping (2-3 business days) is available for an additional fee. We ship nationwide within the United States.

What is your return policy?

+

We offer a 30-day return policy for unused items in their original packaging. If you're not satisfied with your purchase, you can return it for a full refund or exchange. Shipping costs are non-refundable unless the return is due to our error.

Do you offer educational discounts?

+

Yes! We offer a 15% educational discount for teachers, homeschoolers, and educational institutions. Please contact our customer service team with proof of your educational status to receive your discount code.

How do I choose the right game for my child's age?

+

All our products include recommended age ranges, and our product descriptions provide detailed information about skills developed. You can also use our age filter on the shop page or contact us for personalized recommendations based on your child's interests and development level.

Do you offer gift wrapping?

+

Yes! We offer complimentary gift wrapping for all orders. Simply add a note during checkout requesting gift wrapping, and we'll beautifully wrap your purchase and include a personalized gift message at no additional cost.
